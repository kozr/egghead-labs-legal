FAQ

Small answers before you start tracking.

Is the app free?

Yes. Blind Box Tracker is currently a free app for collectors.

Can I request missing series?

Yes. You can suggest collections that are missing so we know what collectors want added next.

Can I track secrets?

Yes. Secret figures, variants, and hard-to-find pulls can be tracked alongside regular checklist items.

Does it support wishlist and trades?

Yes. You can mark wishlist figures and items you are ready to trade. Dedicated duplicate tracking is planned for a future update.

Is this official?

No. Blind Box Tracker is an independent collector tool and is not affiliated with, sponsored by, endorsed by, or officially connected to any collectible manufacturer, brand owner, licensor, retailer, or rights holder unless expressly stated.
